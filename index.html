How to play the game...

  1. The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
  2. You have option to change your symble between (X or O).
  3. Players (You vs Computer) take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
  4. The first player to get 3 of his marks in a (row, column, or diagonal) is the winner.
  5. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has win, the game ends in a tie.
  6. If you choose Continue in case of tie, then the player who has the next turn will start.
  7. In case you choose Continue when any player win (You or Computer), the winner will have the turn to start, and scores will be added.
  8. In case you choose Restart, a new game will start, and all scores will be reset to 0.
